Population Health Mapping


The increasing amount of data describing the health of a given population allows for critical insight into proactive design methedologies in targeting new treatment facilities. This project combines proprietary population health data with census data to understand the socio-economic perspctive of a community's health.

Using an online portal to retrieve census data for users' specfic area of interest, information is fed into a visualization tool for custom datacomparsions. Users have the ability to overlay various populattion health statistics onto regional census data to discover relationships between a partcular heatlh statistic and demographic data
