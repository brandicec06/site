Water Ecologies


Analysis of surface runoff from the Charlottesville airport revealed containments which threatened the health of streams flowing into the Rivanna river. Implementation of a wetland where the airport is currently drained will encourage filtration of chemicals such as potassium acetate and hydrocarbons which degrade the health of adjacent soil and plant systems. Also, the articulation of the drainage process will raise local awareness of human impact on the Rivanna river water system.
