A Tower for Learning


The concept of this project was to create a threshold for the arts common quad, which will contains residence for visiting critics and spaces for informal learning. Views out to the city progress from the scale of the public at the ground floor to the scale of the human on the upper levels. The sequence of vertical space is where students and faculty can interact with one another, because the site has minimal provisions for such venues.
