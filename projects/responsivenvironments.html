Atmospheric Environments


"We engineer environments and objects that sense and respond to their circumstances. Through computationally-enabled objects, we create extra-informative situations to guide customers, users, and future decision-making for our clients. This projects inquires into the balance and unbalanced nature of homeostasis, a condition of ideal balance in organisms. Through the transaction of environmental energies, how can precepts of space be pushed to their limitations."
