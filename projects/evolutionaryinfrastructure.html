Evolutionary Infrastructure


Contemporary habits of urban habitation are increasingly becoming the subject of critical thought in the architectural and urbanism discourse. Fantasies of future cities rendered by our earlier contemporaries dreamed visions of mobile utopias and mechanistic cities which lived and breath, imagining the city as an operative composition for the needs of the common citizen. This project is a transposition of futurist urban visions by Antonio Sant E’lia’s Citta Nuova onto the I-95 corridor spanning Manhatten.
